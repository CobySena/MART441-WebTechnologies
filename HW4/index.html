Ouroboros



You find yourself opening your eyes, and you see that your surroudnings have become an endless expanse of white nothingness, with only a murky white fog clinging to and obscuring the ground. Suddenly, a hooded figure in black appears before you, seemingly out of thin air. All that is visible of the face is the mouth. In a voice that is hard to place both the figure's age and gender, it speaks, 'I shall give you a choice. The Left. The Right. Which would you prefer?' It lifts its hands to show that both of them are curled around something, hiding it from view. Which do you choose?
